FitLife – 7-Day Meal Plan

Beginner-friendly, budget-conscious nutrition

Day 1
  • Breakfast: Oatmeal + milk + banana
  • Lunch: Quinoa bowl (chickpeas + veggies)
  • Dinner: Lentil curry + brown rice
  • Snacks: Yogurt + almonds
Day 2
  • Breakfast: Smoothie (vegan protein + berries)
  • Lunch: Buddha bowl (quinoa + black beans)
  • Dinner: Tofu stir-fry + rice
  • Snacks: Hummus + carrots

Tips

  • Target protein ~1.6–2.2 g/kg/day.
  • Hydration: 2–3 L water daily.
  • Batch-cook grains/legumes to save time & money.
